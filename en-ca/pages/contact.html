Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Is Distilled a drinking game? Can I play it with my kids?

    Distilled is not a drinking game! Distilled is about the science and business of crafting the spirits rather than the consumption of alcohol. So whilst we appreciate that the theme may appeal more to people who do drink alcohol (and may house-rule accordingly!), we also hope that people who don't drink will find the theme interesting and will enjoy the gameplay just as much.

  • Sleeve Size and/or Brand?

    We recommend Mayday Games Sleeves, as we used those when designing the inserts (yes, the insert fits sleeved cards!). Reference total card count and size on the game's page, but the two sleeve sizes you'll need are 56 x 87mm (Standard USA) and 41 x 63mm (Mini USA).

  • My game has damaged or missing components. How can I solve this?

    Please fill out this form to report damaged or missing components in your game.

    Please note: we are unable to provide replacements for free when it concerns aesthetic imperfections or minor damages that don't affect gameplay. Additionally, due to their extremely high replacement and shipping costs, we are unable to provide free box or tray replacements,

  • What fits in the main game box?

    Pretty much everything, including all cards sleeved! This includes the main game, the Africa & Middle East Expansion, and the KS Promo Pack. You can swap out metal coins and cubes and store them as well.

  • How do I re-pack the game once I've punched everything out?

    The game ships with a handy diagram on how to re-pack the game, but Dave has also created a video that walks you through that process.

  • I still have a question!

    If you have a question that hasn't been answered here, but would like to contact someone for an answer, please fill out our contact form above. If it is regarding Distilled rules, consider checking this FAQ on BGG.

Paverson Games

Paverson Games is an indie board game publisher located in Wisconsin, focusing on rich, thematic, and aesthetic game experiences rooted in medium-weight mechanics and strategy.
